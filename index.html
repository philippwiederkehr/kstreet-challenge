RULES

HOW IT WORKS

The KStreet Challenge runs for 3 weeks (Feb 20 – Mar 14, 2026). Complete challenges to earn points and climb the leaderboard!

There are 39 challenges across 5 categories, each worth different point values. Some are easy, some are legendary.

HOW TO SUBMIT

Completed a challenge? Send photo/video proof to the committee via the KStreet WhatsApp group.

The committee will verify and add your completion to the scoreboard. Points only count once confirmed.

SCORING

Each challenge has a fixed point value. Complete as many as you want!

Multiple people can complete the same challenge — everyone gets the points.

Some challenges may have negative points (penalties). Choose wisely!
